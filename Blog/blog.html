My Emulator Adventure

This emulator adventure was filled with awesomeness and entertainment. Since I have BlueStacks, there's no need for me to download it; it was downloaded throughout the summer for me to play Retro Bowl, a football game. Prior to my testing, I have tested out the PaintPot app that was provided on the class website.



Throughout my testing process, I played around the Android simulator for a while. Mainly because I had that experience of using my grandfather's phone that is an HTC. The phone ran on Android Ice Cream Sandwich, which is what I have on BlueStacks. There were some features that I faced such that it took long to respond to my actions. I had to close the application to reset it.


PaintPot was a great app because it lets you do some drawing from three colors: red, blue, and green. Holding it in will draw a point, but clicking the mouse and holding the button will let you draw a line. There is no feature to erase any mistakes, so I had to close the application.
