    COBOL Lotto Winning Ticket sorter

    To help people count out how much money they won on their tickets, they can edit the input file by adding the ID of the ticket and other information. That way, COBOL will sort out if the ticket is a winning or losing ticket. If it's a winning ticket, it will add the amount won to the total. If it's a losing ticket, COBOL will add the information to the losing ticket text file.
